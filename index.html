FAQ
Can I lose my money?
Nope, we don't have to work with your money — all operations and transactions are performed by smart-contract. Your ETH will return at the end of the chosen period.
What if I want my ETH back?
MyEtherHodl is not an investment tool — we help you to save money by holding. To cheer up your motivation to hold, we will take 10% of the whole sum if you want to return your ETH.
What about the future forks?
All tokens from future forks will be saved. In case of, our smart-contract will be in a new net at the same address with the equal balance.
What about AirDrop?
Sorry, no AirDrop. The difference between value and transaction fee is so negligible that we use it for supporting MyEtherHold.
Is it possible to make several holdings from single wallet?
Sure :)
