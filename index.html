Tired of crypto hype? With MyEtherHold you can safely keep your ETH and don't give a fuck about value and market price up to 3 years. It's a formal smart contract, without any fee.
The philosophy of MyEtherHold is similar to Buddhism — if you don't own, it won't own you. Relax guys, that's a joke.
MyEtherHold is a smart contract which allows you to send money to another wallet and get them back when the time is up. The transaction is safe and secure. You can choose 1, 2 or 3 years of saving period. No commission or tricks — the money will come back without any fee. Please, read our documentation for detailed information.
What's the point? To be honest, we are a bit tired of endless crypto talks about market price. Now we have an opportunity to hold our ETH and get them back when the exchange course is higher.
At the end of the term, ETH will be sent back to your eth-address without any commission. Kinda additional motivation for those who give up and cancel the transaction before the end of a term, we'll get a 10% fee. But we are sure you are a strong person.
